Drop Volley

  • Event description
  • Volleyball is a more intense version of "don't let the balloon touch the floor". It's not just a game it's a way to express your Anger without fighting. It is a fun filled event where the participant/team who wins the first round is promoted to the subsequent round.

  • Details
  • 1. It's a two day event.
  • 2. Each team consists of 3 members.
  • 3. Takes place in 3 rounds.

  • Rounds description

  • Round 1: Qualifier Round

    A. One set of 15 points.

    B. 8 team quallifies for round 2.

    C. A team can participate for more than one time to get themselves qualified for second round.

  • Round 2: Prefinal Round

    A. One set of 15 points.

    B. 4 team qualifies for round 3.

  • Round 3: Final Round

    A. Three set of 15 point each.

    B. Congratulation to winner.
